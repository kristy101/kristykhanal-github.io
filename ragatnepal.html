Ragat Nepal

Duration: 15 weeks
Role: Designer
Year: 2022

Project Overview

I designed the UI/UX for Ragat Nepal, a blood-sharing app dedicated to making donor-recipient connections faster and more reliable. The design prioritized simplicity and accessibility, ensuring users could quickly request or donate blood with minimal effort. A streamlined interface guided users through the process, with real-time availability updates and location-based donor matching. To enhance trust and usability, I incorporated verification features and clear visual cues for urgent requests. This project challenged me to think critically about design in high-stakes scenarios, reinforcing the importance of clarity, efficiency, and a user-first approach in digital healthcare solutions.
