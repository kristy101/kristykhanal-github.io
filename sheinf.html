She-Infinity

Duration: 13 weeks
Role: Co-Founder and Designer
Year: 2024

Project Overview

I designed branding, visual assets, and social media content for She-Infinity, a women-led organization dedicated to equipping young people, especially women, with essential skills through hands-on workshops. The branding emphasized empowerment and inclusivity, with a bold yet approachable visual identity that reflected the organization’s mission. I created a cohesive design system, including a distinctive logo, color palette, and typography that conveyed confidence and growth. For social media, I designed engaging posts that highlighted upcoming workshops, success stories, and key insights, ensuring consistency in tone and aesthetics. This project allowed me to craft a strong, purpose-driven brand presence that resonated with the community and effectively communicated She-Infinity’s impact.
