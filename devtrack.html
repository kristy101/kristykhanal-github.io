DevTrack

Duration: 8 weeks
Role: Designer & Developer
Year: 2024

Project Overview

DevTrack is an innovative web application designed to bridge the communication gap by bringing transparency and accountability to government projects in Kathmandu Metropolitan City (KMC). It provides comprehensive details on project budgets, quality, schedules, authorities and usage, ensuring the general public has easy access to vital data. This transparency builds trust between the KMC government and its citizens , reducing corruption and mismanagement while promoting a culture of openness . Additionally DevTrack’s real time monitoring feature allows citizens to track project progress , hold authorities accountable for delays , and facilitate better communication and engagement . Moreover , DevTrack includes a public forum where users can publicly comment on development projects and engage in discussions . This forum empowers citizens to share their views and feedback directly with authorities and other stakeholders . Users will also have access to detailed information about development initiatives specifically targeted at them , enabling them to understand and influence the projects that impact their communities directly . This dual functionality ensures that DevTrack not only provides transparency but also promotes active citizen participation in the development.
